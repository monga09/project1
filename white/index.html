Products

WhiteHaX Cyber Readiness Verification: Trusted by some of the largest Cyber Insurance Carriers with Tens of thousands of licenses deployed, WhiteHaX is a cloud-hosted, automated, cyber-readiness verification (pen-testing) platform. The WhiteHaX cyber-insurance version provides a no-install, no-impact, quick (under 15-min) verification of a business' cyber-readiness by simulating several threat scenarios against the business' deployed security infrastructure, including network perimeter defenses and endpoint security & controls. Simulated threat scenarios include firewall attacks, user-attacks from internet such as drive-by downloads, email phishing/spoofing/spamming, ransomware, data-exfiltration attempts and others. The 3 types of network scans allow businesses to verify potential dangers of malware/ransomware propagation, wifi security and external scan of network firewalls. WhiteHaX Cyber readiness verification allows business to stay ahead of potential vulnerablities in the business security infrastructure.

****

WhiteHaX PhishSim Platform: WhiteHaX PhishSim is complete cloud-based platform for training, simulation and verification of business employees on the risks of Phishing and how to detect and block Phishing scams. Training is performed using interactive tutorials, games and quizzes. Verification is done through simulating hundreds of actual, real-life Phishing Scams from Microsoft Office365 to Banking, HR, corporate and other scams to verify employee readiness against phishing. Everything from whether the employee completed training, played games, took quizzes to whether they opened phishing emails, clicked any embedded URL links or attachments and entered any prsonal data, is all tracked. Comprehensive reports then allow CIO/CSO to verify the business readiness against Phishing & where the business might be vulnerable by groups or individual users. Click Here to Learn More...

WhiteHaX CyberSafe Apps: WhiteHaX CyberSafe Mobile Apps are a one-of-a-kind, cloud-managed mobile apps for protecting mobile devices by first performing a thorough verification of a mobile device cyber-readiness, its OS and App settings and network security against several threat scenarios. They offer a wholistic approach to complete mobile security and data privacy with features such as. Deep Analysis of Security & Data Privacy; On-demand WiFi Security Scan; One-click, Fast, AES-265 secured, Cloud-based VPN; Network scan for Wifi-connected smart-device in the home/business; Family Protect Features to block inappropriate content; Web Phishing Filter to eliminate risks of bad sites; Cross-platform Password Vault and Comprehensive Security Training on device security, social media and other commonly used apps. WhiteHaX PhishSim for Enterprises.

Train Employees on Phishin Risks

User error thru Phishing scams is the no. 1 reason businesses get infected with malware/ransomware or have other types of cyber breaches. Train employees using comprehensive, interactive trainings, games and quizzes on Web or on their mobile devices through out the whole year - for less than a cup of coffee.

Verify Phishing Readiness

Once the employees are trained, verify their phishing readiness by periodically sending simulated, real-life phishing emails to see if any of them fall for these scams by clicking URL links, attachments or entering data in fake web-sites. WhiteHaX PhishSim platform has hundreds of built-in real-life phishing scam templates that are constantly updated to include new, latest phishing attacks.

Measure Phishing Readiness & Vulnerabilities

All user activity through trainings and simulated emails are tracked and reported in to central database. Comprehensive reports show phsihing readiness of the overall business, by groups or individual employees. CIO/CISO can track phishing vulnerablities across the business and help eliminate phishing risks.
